About Us

Our Story

A passionate team dedicated to creating magical gaming experiences that bring joy to players around the world.

Our Mission

At attilano, our mission is simple yet profound: we want to create games that make people smile. We believe that mobile games should be more than just a way to pass the time—they should be memorable experiences that brighten your day, challenge your mind, and bring a touch of magic to everyday life.

We are committed to developing family-friendly content that can be enjoyed by players of all ages. Every character we design, every puzzle we craft, and every world we build is done with the utmost care and attention to detail. We want parents to feel confident that their children are playing safe, wholesome games, while adults can rediscover the joy of simple, delightful entertainment.

Our Disney-inspired aesthetic combines colorful visuals, charming characters, and engaging gameplay to create experiences that feel both timeless and fresh. We draw inspiration from classic animation, whimsical fairy tales, and the natural wonder of the world around us.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at attilano, from the games we create to the way we interact with our community.

Safety First

Child safety is our top priority. All our games are designed with family-friendly content, age-appropriate gameplay, and strict content guidelines to ensure a safe environment for young players.

Passion & Quality

We pour our hearts into every game. From concept to launch, each title undergoes rigorous testing and refinement to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and entertainment value.

Community Driven

Our players are at the heart of everything we do. We actively listen to feedback, engage with our community, and continuously improve our games based on what our players love and want.

Global Inclusion

We create games for everyone, everywhere. Our titles are available in multiple languages and are designed to be accessible to players from all backgrounds and abilities across the globe.

Continuous Innovation

While we honor classic game design, we're always exploring new ideas, mechanics, and technologies to keep our games fresh, exciting, and at the forefront of mobile entertainment.

Transparency

We believe in honest, clear communication with our players. From in-app purchases to data collection, we're upfront about how our games work and how we handle your information.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to serving millions of players worldwide, here's how attilano has grown.

The Beginning

attilano was founded with a simple dream: to create mobile games that bring genuine happiness to players. What started as a small team with big ideas has grown into a passionate studio dedicated to magical gaming experiences.

First Launch

Our debut game launched on both iOS and Android, introducing players to our charming world of cute characters and engaging puzzles. The positive reception validated our vision and inspired us to create more.

Growing Community

As our game library expanded, so did our global family of players. We reached milestone after milestone, with each achievement reminding us why we started this journey in the first place.

Today & Beyond

Today, we continue to innovate and inspire. With exciting new titles in development and a growing team of talented creators, the future of attilano looks brighter than ever.
