Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Just reach out and we'll be happy to help!

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues with our games, please contact us through this page with the subject "Report a Bug." Include your device type, operating system version, and a detailed description of the issue. Screenshots are also helpful!

Are your games free?

Many of our games offer free-to-play experiences with optional in-app purchases. We believe in providing quality entertainment that's accessible to everyone, while offering optional premium content for those who want to enhance their experience.

Can I work at attilano?

We're always on the lookout for talented, passionate people! If you think you'd be a great fit for our team, send us a message with your portfolio or resume. We can't wait to hear from you!

How can I partner with attilano?

We love collaborating with brands, influencers, and organizations that share our values. For partnership inquiries, please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the subject dropdown and tell us about your collaboration idea!

What age rating are your games?

Our games are designed to be family-friendly and suitable for players of all ages. Most of our titles are rated for everyone, though specific ratings may vary by region and app store. Check the individual game pages for detailed age ratings.

How long until I get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters or technical issues, please be descriptive in your message so we can assist you quickly and effectively.
